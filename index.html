Muscle Wins!: Treatment in Clinical Orthodontics by Etsuko Kondo

“This outstanding volume by Dr. Etsuko Kondo shows that she is not only a superb clinician, producing magnificent results, but she also does this, recognizing the fundamental laws of anatomy and physiology and the effects of respiration on the ultimate position of the teeth, jaws and facial structural balance of all tissues. As she says, “I learned early that it is important to pay much attention to functional recovery of abnormal tongue, perioral muscle, chewing and cervical muscle activities, as well as respiration, in correcting malocclusion.”
